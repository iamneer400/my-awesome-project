About me

🌀

Who is Maya?

I'm a graphic designer & visual artist based between Barcelona and Mexico City. I believe in bold color, radical honesty, and designs that evoke emotion. From experimental typography to immersive branding — I make the unseen felt.

✨

Philosophy

My work breaks boundaries between minimalism and electric expression. No beige, no gray — only stories told in neons, gradients, and fearless composition. Let's disrupt the ordinary.

🌍

Global Footprint

Exhibited in CDMX, Berlin, and Seoul. Clients range from indie music labels to sustainable streetwear. I travel light but create loud.
