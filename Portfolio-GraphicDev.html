Who is Chad?

Chad Gibbons is a multidisciplinary graphic designer blending brutalist simplicity with narrative depth. Raised between New York and Paris, his work captures raw emotion and cultural dialogue.

With a passion for minimalistic yet powerful compositions, Chad has collaborated with independent brands, bars, and film societies across the US and Europe. His approach merges classic typography with modern edge — always in black & white, always timeless.
